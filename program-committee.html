4th ACM SIGSPATIAL International Workshop on Geospatial Humanities

Co-located with ACM SIGSPATIAL 2020 in Seattle, Washington, USA

Program Committee

The 4th ACM SIGSPATIAL International Workshop on Geospatial Humanities (GeoHumanities 2020) aims to bring together researchers and practitioners from different sub-fields of computer science and the geographical information sciences, interested in the application of spatial methods and technology to the humanities.

Our program commitee draws from multiple disciplines, including computer science and engineering, GIScience, digital humanities, and others.
